# 🇳🇬 Nigeria Food Insecurity Crisis — Data Analysis 2022–2025

## Project Overview
A state-by-state Excel analysis of Nigeria's acute food insecurity crisis 
across all 36 states and FCT, using official Cadre Harmonisé data published 
by the Nigerian Government, FAO, and WFP. Rather than working with a 
simulated dataset, this project compiles real figures from published 
humanitarian assessments to answer four practical questions that 
policymakers and NGOs actually need answered.

## Key Findings
- 🔴 33.1 million Nigerians projected food insecure in 2025 lean season
- 📈 71% rise in food insecurity nationally from 2022 to 2025
- ⚔️ Conflict affects more people than Inflation, Flooding & Drought combined
- 👶 890,000 children under 5 face acute malnutrition in Borno alone
